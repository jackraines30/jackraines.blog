Three-day weekends predicted to become the norm

CULTURE Apr 4, 2024

Steve Cohen, founder of Point72 and owner of the Mets, said he believes multiple forces, including artificial intelligence, have made a four-day work week imminent. If we look at the data, there are other signs the shift is starting.

The Wall Street Journal recently reported that the average signoff time on Fridays has steadily trickled down to 4:03 PM, and Friday is by far the most popular WFH day for hybrid employees.

AI doomers have long worried that artificial intelligence would take all our jobs, but labor data may be starting to show that technological improvements could give us our Fridays back.
