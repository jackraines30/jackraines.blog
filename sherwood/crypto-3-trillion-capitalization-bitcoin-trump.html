Crypto's market cap passes $3 trillion

CRYPTO Nov 14, 2024

The total cryptocurrency market capitalization broke $3 trillion on November 14, reaching a high of nearly $3.2 trillion, as investor optimism for a crypto-friendly Trump administration continues to send prices for coins like bitcoin higher. It ’ s not just the coins that are benefiting, either. Crypto trading platform Coinbase is up 57% since November 4 as well, as investors believe the uptick in trading activity will be a tailwind for the exchange.
