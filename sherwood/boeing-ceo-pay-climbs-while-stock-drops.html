Boeing CEO Pay Climbs While Stock Drops

BUSINESS Apr 8, 2024

Boeing stock might be down 58% from its all-time high in March 2019, but you wouldn't know it by looking at the company's CEO compensation history.

Despite Boeing's struggles, the airplane manufacturer has increased its CEO compensation by 129% since 2019, from $14.3 million up to $32.8 million in 2023.

Current CEO Dave Calhoun is stepping down at the end of 2024, but Reuters reported that his potential retirement payout was worth up to $44 million according to a 2023 regulatory filing.
