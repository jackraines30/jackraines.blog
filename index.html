I'm a writer and investor based in New York City. I've been blogging on Substack since 2021, and my writing covers personal finance, investing, and the general chaos of being in your twenties. In 2025, I signed with Penguin Random House to write Young Money, a field guide to wealth and purpose in your twenties.

By day, I'm an investor at Slow Ventures, where I split my time between our generalist seed fund and our Creator Fund.

If any of that sounds interesting, poke around. If you want to reach me, the best place is Twitter/X or via email at jack@youngmoney.co.
